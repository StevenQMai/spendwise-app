## About SpendWise

SpendWise is an innovative expense management application that revolutionizes how travelers track, manage, and optimize their travel expenses. Whether you're embarking on a solo adventure or coordinating expenses for a group trip, SpendWise provides the tools you need to stay organized and within budget.

## Project Goals

SpendWise aims to:

- **Simplify Expense Management**: Streamline the process of tracking and managing travel expenses for individuals and groups
- **Enhance Financial Awareness**: Provide insights and analytics to help users make better financial decisions during travel
- **Facilitate Group Coordination**: Enable seamless expense sharing and budget management for group trips
- **Leverage AI Technology**: Utilize artificial intelligence to provide personalized recommendations and automated expense categorization

## Target Audience

- **Solo Travelers**: Individuals who want to track and optimize their personal travel expenses
- **Group Travelers**: Friends, families, or colleagues traveling together who need to manage shared expenses
- **Budget-Conscious Travelers**: Users who want to maintain financial discipline while exploring the world
